Upcoming Events

  • March 4

    Bark and Brew

    Oviedo Bark & Brew is a free, fun, family-friendly festival with over 30 styles of beer, lots of food vendors, pet related vendors, live entertainment and other shows, plus a new VIP Craft Beer & Cheese Pairing inside the Cultural Center.

  • March 20

    DOG 4 A DAY

    A simple volunteer service for anyone. You can come to the rescue center and take an animal off property for the day. A lot of volunteers will take our dogs to a park, beach, and mini adventures away from the rescue. It’s great for you and for the dogs!

  • March 21

    9th Annual Sanford Pints N’ Paws Craft Beer Festival

    Unfortunately we are going to need to postpone the 9th Annual Pints n Paws Craft Beer Festival event because of the Coronavirus (COVID-19) crisis. We know it hurts as many of us truly love this event but as you can see events across the world are being canceled/postponed. We will be keeping a close eye on the situation and staying in constant communication with the city and county and at first opportunity we will be RESCHEDULING the event!

  • Nov 15

    Barktoberfest

    A rescue appreciation event to celebrate the partnership between Orange County Animal Services and the rescue community. Animal Services works with more than 100 rescue groups that aid in the recovery and rehabilitation of many homeless animals. Barktoberfest allows these animals an opportunity to find new forever homes. The event also features pet related vendors and food trucks. Northeast lawn at Lake Eola.
