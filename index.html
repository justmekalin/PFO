    Barktoberfest

    A rescue appreciation event to celebrate the partnership between Orange County Animal Services and the rescue community. Animal Services works with more than 100 rescue groups that aid in the recovery and rehabilitation of many homeless animals. Barktoberfest allows these animals an opportunity to find new forever homes. The event also features pet related vendors and food trucks. Northeast lawn at Lake Eola.

How We Help

Paws for Orlando is a community of dedicated citizens who are bringing awareness and support to dog shelters in the Orlando area. We provide service opportunities by connecting those who are looking to volunteer, donate, foster, or adopt dogs through our partners: Pet Rescue By Judy, Orange County Animal Services, and Pet Alliance of Greater Orlando.

  • Bring awareness and support to dog shelters in need
  • Providing supplies for dogs and kennels
  • Organizing movement of dogs from overcrowded or kill shelters to better shelters
  • Connecting the community to local shelters
